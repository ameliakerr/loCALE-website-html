Emily Weisburst

Associate Professor at the University of California, Los Angeles

I am a labor and public economist who focuses on topics including criminal justice/legal policy, policing, education, and immigration. I am an Associate Professor of Public Policy at the University of California, Los Angeles, in the Luskin School of Public Affairs. I am also an National Bureau of Economics Research (NBER) Faculty Research Fellow, a Research Affiliate at the Institute of Labor Economics (IZA), an invited researcher at the Abdul Latif Jameel Poverty Action Lab (J-PAL), and have affiliations with the California Policy Lab (CPL) and the California Center for Population Research (CCPR).
