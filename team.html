The People Behind LoCALE

Our Team

LoCALE is led by a team of economists specializing in criminal justice, policing, and public policy.

Lead Researchers

Emily Weisburst

Emily Weisburst is a labor and public economist whose research focuses on criminal justice and legal policy, policing, education, and immigration. She is an Associate Professor of Public Policy at the University of California, Los Angeles, Luskin School of Public Affairs.

Felipe Goncalves

Felipe Goncalves is a labor and public economist whose research focuses on policing, crime, and education. He is an Assistant Professor of Economics at the University of California, Los Angeles.

Brittany Street

Brittany Street is a labor and public economist whose research focuses on the economics of crime and the criminal justice system. She is an Assistant Professor of Economics at the University of Missouri and Associate Director of the Economic and Policy Analysis Research Center (EPARC).
