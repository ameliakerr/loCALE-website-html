Overview

About the Data

The LoCALE database compiles harmonized micro-level arrest, incident, and dispatch records from thousands of municipalities across the United States. To address inconsistencies across agencies, LoCALE standardizes offense classifications and harmonizes variable definitions to ensure comparability across cities and over time.

Detailed documentation of variable construction and data processing decisions is available in the accompanying codebooks.
