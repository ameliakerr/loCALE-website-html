Data Information

The LoCALE database compiles harmonized micro-level arrest, incident, and dispatch records from thousands of municipalities across the United States. To address inconsistencies across agencies, LoCALE standardizes offense classifications and harmonizes variable definitions to ensure comparability across cities and over time. Detailed documentation of variable construction and data processing decisions is available in the accompanying codebooks.

Information on geographic coverage, time periods, and data access procedures is provided below.

  • Arrest: Records of individuals who have been apprehended by law enforcement officers.
  • Incident: Reports of crime events that require police attention.
